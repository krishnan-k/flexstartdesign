F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What industries do you serve?

We serve Automotive, FMCG, Electronics, Packaging, Pharmaceuticals, Food, and Special Application industries. Our solutions are designed for Indian shop-floor conditions and have been deployed across 100+ customers.

Q2: What makes your machine vision different from off-the-shelf solutions?

We co-design every system with the customer. Both hardware and software are built in-house — no finger-pointing between vendors. Our team has hands-on shop-floor experience and builds practical, affordable solutions tailored to real manufacturing environments.

Q3: Do you integrate with existing ERP/MES systems?

Yes. Our platform is machine-agnostic and PLC-agnostic. We build a harmonization layer over existing IT systems — connecting ERP, CRM, MES, and LIMS into a single unified data governance layer without requiring a full system replacement.

Q4: What support do you offer after deployment?

We offer remote diagnostics, on-site training, and lifetime upgrade options. We serve startups and large OEMs alike with flexible commercial models — so support is tailored to your scale and budget.

Q5: What measurable impact can we expect?

Our deployments have delivered: 40% reduction in overall cycle time, 85% reduction in defect escape rates, 60% less manpower needed for inspection, 100% detection accuracy on metal insert checks, and zero customer complaints for 2 consecutive quarters.

Q6: Do you work with small factories or only large plants?

We serve startups and OEMs alike with flexible commercial models. Our solutions are specifically built for Indian MSMEs as well as Tier 1 and Tier 2 automotive suppliers.
